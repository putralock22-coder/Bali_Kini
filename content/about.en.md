## Bali's Daily Data & News Portal

**Bali Kini** (balikini.id) is an information portal presenting up-to-date data and daily news about the Island of the Gods. We collect, analyse, and present data from official sources in a format accessible to everyone — Balinese residents, tourists, researchers, and investors alike.

## Our Mission

> *Making Bali's data easy to access, easy to understand, and useful for everyone.*

Bali is one of the world's most important tourism destinations, yet data about its economic conditions, weather, and culture is often scattered across multiple sources and difficult for the general public to access. Bali Kini bridges that gap.

## What We Cover

### 📊 Real-time Data
- **Tourism**: International visitor statistics from BPS Bali, hotel data, and tourism trends
- **Economy**: GDP, inflation, Bank Indonesia data, and regional economic indicators
- **Weather**: Forecasts and warnings from BMKG Meteorological Station Denpasar
- **Events & Culture**: Official event calendar from Bali Calendar of Events

### 📰 Articles & Analysis
Articles published daily — breaking news, travel guides, economic analysis, and Balinese cultural stories.

### 🌐 Multilingual
Available in **Bahasa Indonesia**, **English**, and **中文 (Mandarin)** to serve visitors from around the world.

## Data Sources

Bali Kini uses only official and verified data sources:

| Source | Data |
|--------|------|
| **BPS Bali** | Visitor statistics, inflation, GDP |
| **BMKG Station III** | Weather, waves, climate |
| **Bali Tourism Office** | Tourism data, events |
| **Bank Indonesia Bali** | Inflation, regional economy |
| **Bali Provincial Government** | Policy, government programmes |
