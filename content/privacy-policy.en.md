Welcome to **Bali Kini** (balikini.id). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

## 1. Information We Collect

Bali Kini is a static news and data portal. We do **not** directly collect personal information such as names, emails, or passwords.

Data automatically collected by third-party services includes:
- **Anonymous usage data**: pages visited, visit duration, device type, and approximate location (via Google Analytics)
- **Cookies**: used by Google Analytics and Google AdSense for a more relevant experience

## 2. Use of Cookies

Bali Kini uses cookies from third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Type |
|---------|---------|------|
| Google Analytics (GA4) | Anonymous visitor statistics | Analytics |
| Google AdSense | Relevant advertising | Advertising |
| OneSignal | Browser notifications (optional) | Functional |

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

## 3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with the site. Data collected is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking: [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

## 4. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.

To manage your Google ad preferences: [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com)

## 5. Third-Party Services

Bali Kini links to external sources (BPS, BMKG, Bank Indonesia, etc.) for the data displayed. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party sites.

## 6. Data Security

Bali Kini is a static website. We do not maintain user databases. All data transmission is protected by HTTPS/TLS encryption.

## 7. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy regulations, you have the right to:
- Know what data is collected about you
- Request deletion of your data (for data collected via third-party services, please contact those providers directly)
- Opt out of analytics tracking and advertising
