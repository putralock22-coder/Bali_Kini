The Festival of Arts, Ornaments, and Distinctiveness of Seririt District — better known as **Fesona Seririt 2026** — was officially opened on Wednesday, June 3, 2026 at Lapangan Umum Seririt in Buleleng Regency. The opening ceremony was a spectacular affair featuring **425 colossal dancers** who mesmerized thousands of attendees.

## Theme "Abhinaya Kumala": An Expression of Cultural Beauty

This year's festival carries the theme **"Abhinaya Kumala"**, a Sanskrit phrase meaning *the expression of beauty*. The theme reflects the spirit of the Seririt community to showcase its rich artistic traditions passed down through generations, while affirming cultural identity in an era of rapid modernization.

**Buleleng Regent I Nyoman Sutjidra**, accompanied by **Deputy Regent Gede Supriatna**, was present in person to officially open the festival. The delegation was welcomed with a Hanoman Dance performance and a dramatic torch procession before entering the main arena.

## A Spectacular Opening Ceremony

The highlight of the opening was the simultaneous performance of **425 colossal dancers** presenting two iconic traditional dances: **Panjembrahma Dance** and **Tari Dewi Danuh** — the signature dance of Seririt District. Thousands of spectators packed into the field were mesmerized by the synchronized movements and vibrant traditional costumes filling the stage.

The full opening procession included:

- **Hanoman Dance welcome** upon the Regent's arrival
- **Torch procession** by festival participants from all villages in Seririt District
- **Panjembrahma Dance** featuring hundreds of dancers in traditional attire
- **Tari Dewi Danuh** as Seririt's iconic cultural performance
- **Arts exhibition, ornaments**, and local MSME product showcases

## Schedule and Program: June 3–7, 2026

Fesona Seririt 2026 runs for five days, from **June 3 to 7, 2026**, with a full program of activities involving all segments of society:

| Date    | Main Activity |
|---------|--------------|
| June 3  | Official opening & 425-dancer colossal performance |
| June 4  | Traditional arts competition between villages |
| June 5  | MSME exhibition and local crafts fair |
| June 6  | Contemporary and traditional performing arts |
| June 7  | Closing ceremony and award announcements |

## Economic Impact for Local MSMEs

Beyond a celebration of the arts, Fesona Seririt 2026 is designed as an **engine for the creative economy** in Buleleng. The thousands of visitors attending each day create major opportunities for Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) to promote and sell their local products.

In his opening remarks, Regent Sutjidra emphasized that festivals like Fesona Seririt are not just entertainment platforms — they are **arenas for community economic empowerment**. Local food vendors, artisans, and performers all benefit from direct exposure to thousands of potential customers.

## Preserving Culture in the Modern Age

Fesona Seririt stands as a living testament to Bali's enduring commitment to cultural preservation. In Buleleng Regency alone, a series of similar festivals — including Festasada, Busungbiu Festival, and Singa Kren Festival — are held throughout the year to safeguard ancestral traditions.

"This festival is a platform for communities to demonstrate their regional potential while driving economic growth through MSMEs and the tourism sector," said the festival organizers.

With overwhelming public enthusiasm from the very first day, Fesona Seririt 2026 is set to become an important milestone in Bali's cultural calendar — a celebration that not only preserves local identity but also opens doors to meaningful cultural tourism across the Buleleng region.
