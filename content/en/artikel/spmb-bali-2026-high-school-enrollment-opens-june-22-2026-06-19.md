In less than three days, Bali's public high schools and vocational schools will officially open their doors to new students. The **Sistem Penerimaan Murid Baru (SPMB)** — Bali's new school enrollment system — for the 2026/2027 academic year launches on **June 22, 2026**, giving prospective students and parents little time to prepare their documents.

## Two Registration Stages

The SPMB 2026 is structured across two distinct stages targeting different selection pathways:

### Stage 1 — Affirmative Action, Relocation, and Achievement Pathways
- **Registration**: June 22–24, 2026 (08:00–18:00 WITA)
- **Document verification**: until June 28, 2026
- **Results announcement**: June 29, 2026 at 17:00 WITA

### Stage 2 — Domicile Pathway
- **Registration**: June 30 – July 2, 2026 (08:00–18:00 WITA)
- **Document verification**: until July 8, 2026
- **Results announcement**: July 9, 2026 at 17:00 WITA
- **Re-registration for accepted students**: July 10–12, 2026

## 13 Available Selection Pathways

SPMB 2026 offers **10 main pathways** plus **3 domicile pathways**, giving students from diverse backgrounds a fair shot at admission:

**Affirmative Action Pathways:**
- Inclusion (students with special needs)
- Economically Disadvantaged Families (KETM)

**Relocation Pathways:**
- Children of Teachers/Education Staff
- Parental Duty Transfer (job relocation)

**Achievement Pathways:**
- Leadership Achievement
- Academic Achievement in Science and Research Technology
- Non-Academic Achievement in Sports
- Non-Academic Achievement in Balinese Language, Arts & Culture
- Non-Academic Achievement in Non-Balinese Language, Arts & Culture
- Academic Ability Achievement

**Domicile Pathways:**
- Schools with Partnership Agreements
- Krama Desa Adat (traditional village community members)
- Residency-Based

## General Requirements

All prospective students applying for Year 10 (Grade 10) at either SMA or SMK must meet the following criteria:

- **Age**: No older than 21 years as of July 1, 2026
- **Education**: Must have completed junior high school (SMP/MTs/Paket B equivalent)
- **Declaration letter** signed by a parent or legal guardian
- Valid **diploma or Certificate of Graduation (SKL)**

### Special Requirements for Certain Vocational Programs

Students applying to SMK programs in **pharmacy, hospitality, culinary arts, and technology disciplines** must additionally submit a **doctor's certificate confirming they are not color blind**. This requirement ensures students can safely perform practical tasks in these fields.

## The SPMB Philosophy: Quality, Equity, and Character

Bali's 2026 SPMB is anchored in three core principles: **input quality, equitable access, and character development rooted in Balinese culture**. This approach reflects Bali's educational policy direction, which aims to develop not just academically capable students, but well-rounded individuals grounded in local values.

### Breaking the "Favorite School" Stigma

The Bali Regional Legislature (DPRD) has also pushed for a shift in mindset about school selection. The SPMB system is expected to help dismantle the longstanding "favorite vs. non-favorite school" stigma, with a more transparent and pathway-diverse selection process encouraging students to distribute more evenly across all schools in the province.

## How to Apply

Registration is conducted **online** through the official Bali Province SPMB portal. Students and parents are strongly advised to gather all required documents now — including junior high school diplomas, birth certificates, family registration cards, and any special documentation — well before June 22, 2026.
