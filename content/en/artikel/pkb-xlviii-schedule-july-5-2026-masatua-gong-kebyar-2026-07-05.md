Sunday, July 5, 2026, brings another vibrant day of performances to the **48th Bali Arts Festival (PKB XLVIII)** at Art Center Denpasar. From midday through late evening, the compound transforms into a living stage where local, national, and international art forms converge in celebration of Balinese culture.

## Masatua Bali Competition Opens the Day

The day kicks off at **10:00 AM (WITA)** with the **Wimbakara (Competition) Masatua Bali** at Kalangan Angsoka. Masatua is the ancient Balinese oral storytelling tradition—tales passed down through generations in the Balinese language. This competition brings together storytellers from across the island, keeping a living heritage alive and introducing younger audiences to the richness of ancestral language and narrative.

## National Arts Take the Stage

At **2:00 PM**, the Lembaga Kesenian Bali Yayasan Saraswati from Jakarta performs at Kalangan Ayodya—a reminder that Balinese art thrives far beyond the island's shores.

**3:00 PM** at Gedung Ksirarnawa features the **Bali World Culture Celebration (BWCC)** with Sanggar Tari Bali Wira Kencana from DKI Jakarta, showcasing how diaspora communities keep Balinese dance traditions alive in the capital.

At **5:00 PM**, the stage at Kalangan Angsoka shifts registers entirely with a performance of **Traditional Sundanese Dance** by Viatikara from Bandung, West Java. The inclusion of non-Balinese art forms reflects the festival's broader spirit of *Atma Kerthi*—the theme of PKB XLVIII celebrating the living soul of culture, open to all traditions of the archipelago.

## Evening Highlights: Legong & Gong Kebyar Dewasa

The evening opens at **7:00 PM** at Kalangan Ayodya with a special tribute performance: **Legong Style of the Late Maestro Men Sana**, brought to life by Sekaa Andir Usana Budaya. The late palegongan master Andir Tista's legacy lives on through this rare and graceful presentation.

Simultaneously at **7:30 PM**, a **Wayang Kulit Parade** unfolds in front of Gedung Kriya, featuring master dalang (puppet masters) performing scenes from the great Balinese epics.

The night's centerpiece begins at **7:30 PM** on the Open Stage of Ardha Candra: the **Gong Kebyar Dewasa Competition** pitting:

- **Sekaa Gong Tri Tunggal** — representing the City of Denpasar
- **Sekaa Gong Kebyar Widya Taruna** — representing Jembrana Regency

This head-to-head gamelan competition is one of the most anticipated events at PKB, drawing thousands of spectators who come to witness the precision, coordination, and artistry of both ensembles.

## PKB XLVIII in Its Final Week

PKB XLVIII 2026 opened on June 13 and closes on **July 11, 2026**. As the festival enters its final stretch, every performance carries added significance. The festival features:

- **10 international art groups** from New York, China, South Korea, Japan, India, Hong Kong, and Timor-Leste—totaling approximately **260 international artists**
- **955 participants from across the Indonesian archipelago**, representing Jakarta, East Java, Central Java, West Java, Yogyakarta, Banten, and Central Kalimantan

All performances at Art Center Denpasar are **free and open to the public**. The venue is located on Jalan Nusa Indah, Denpasar.
