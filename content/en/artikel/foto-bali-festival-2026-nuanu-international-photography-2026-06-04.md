Bali has once again proven itself as a global magnet for art and culture. On June 3, 2026, the **FOTO Bali Festival 2026** was officially launched at **Nuanu Creative City**, an integrated creative hub in Tabanan, Bali. This second edition of the international photography festival carries the theme **"Afterimage"** and runs for 40 days through **July 12, 2026**.

## 36 Artists from 24 Countries Selected

What makes FOTO Bali Festival 2026 truly remarkable is the rigorous selection process its participants went through. Via an **international open call**, the organizers received nearly **700 applications from more than 80 countries** worldwide — a figure that demonstrates the enormous enthusiasm of the global photography community for this festival.

From hundreds of applicants, only **36 artists from 24 countries** were selected to showcase their best works. They present in a variety of formats, from solo photography exhibitions and multimedia art installations to cross-cultural collaborations that enrich visitors' perspectives.

## Theme "Afterimage": Reflecting on Life After the Photo

The theme "Afterimage" chosen for the 2026 edition carries profound meaning. The festival invites all parties — artists, visitors, and the general public — to contemplate **what remains after a photograph is taken**.

Beyond the printed image, this theme questions how a photo is subsequently edited, composed, contextualized, published, archived, shared, and ultimately remembered. In today's digital age, where millions of photos are taken every second, these questions become increasingly relevant and urgent.

## Nuanu Creative City: A Stage for Global Creativity

The choice of **Nuanu Creative City** in Tabanan as the festival's main venue is no coincidence. This thriving creative district offers the facilities and exhibition spaces needed to host an internationally acclaimed festival.

Several iconic locations within Nuanu serve as stages for the artists' works:

- **Labyrinth Art Gallery** — main exhibition of photography works
- **Labyrinth Dome** — installations and multimedia art projections
- **Japanese Garden** — outdoor exhibitions and site-specific works
- **Block 42** — discussions, workshops, and public programs

### 40-Day Program Lineup

Throughout the festival period, visitors can enjoy a diverse range of programs designed for all audiences:

- **Photography exhibitions** featuring the 36 selected artists
- **Discussions and talks** with artists, curators, and international photography experts
- **Workshops** for both professional and aspiring photographers
- **Film screenings** and documentaries related to photography
- **Outdoor projections** in Nuanu's open spaces
- **Public programs** open to students, academics, and local communities

## Bali as a Global Creative Hub

FOTO Bali Festival 2026 is more than just an art exhibition. It is part of a larger mission: **establishing Bali as a global center for photography and visual arts**. By bringing together artists from across the world, the festival creates a shared space for productive cross-cultural dialogue.

This second edition also demonstrates that FOTO Bali Festival has grown into one of Southeast Asia's most prestigious photography festivals. The success of the inaugural edition in building networks among artists, cultural institutions, and local communities has laid a strong foundation for this year's event.

For Tabanan itself, the festival delivers real economic benefits through increased visits from both domestic and international tourists who travel specifically for the event, while also elevating Nuanu's profile as a world-class cultural tourism destination.

## Visitor Information

**FOTO Bali Festival 2026** is open to the public from **June 3 to July 12, 2026** at Nuanu Creative City, Tabanan, Bali. Art enthusiasts, photographers, tourists, students, and the general public are all welcome to attend and become part of this global photography conversation taking place in the heart of the Island of the Gods.
