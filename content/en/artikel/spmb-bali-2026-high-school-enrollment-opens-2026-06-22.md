## New Student Enrollment Opens Across Bali

Monday, June 22, 2026 — Bali Province officially launched the **New Student Admission System (SPMB)** for senior high schools (SMA) and vocational high schools (SMK) for the 2026/2027 academic year. Registration opens simultaneously online through the official government portal at **bali.spmb.id**, accessible to all prospective students across Bali.

The Bali Provincial Education, Youth, and Sports Office (Disdikpora) confirmed that the online registration system is operating smoothly. However, the agency cautioned that internet connectivity issues in remote areas could pose challenges, recommending students in rural regions register early or use internet facilities at their current school.

## Three Selection Pathways and Registration Schedule

SPMB 2026/2027 in Bali offers three main selection pathways for SMA/SMK admission, each with its own registration window:

### Achievement Pathway (June 22–24, 2026)
Open to students with notable academic or non-academic achievements, including sports competitions, arts awards, and science olympiads. Applicants must submit verified certificates or achievement citations endorsed by their current school.

### Affirmation and Transfer Pathway (June 29–30, 2026)
The affirmation route provides opportunities for students from low-income households holding the Indonesia Smart Card (KIP) or registered in social assistance programs. The transfer pathway caters specifically to children of civil servants (ASN), military (TNI), and police (Polri) personnel who have been recently reassigned to Bali.

### Domicile Pathway (July 1–4, 2026)
Selection is based on the proximity of a student's home to the preferred school. Applicants must prove their domicile through a Family Card (Kartu Keluarga / KK) issued no later than one year prior to the registration opening date.

## Elementary School Enrollment Also Opens

Alongside SMA/SMK registration, the SPMB process for elementary school (SD) enrollment also begins today, running from **June 22 to 25, 2026**, allowing families with children at multiple school levels to handle their paperwork within the same period.

## Non-Discrimination Principles at the Core

The 2026/2027 SPMB process in Bali is built on a commitment to **non-discrimination**, placing a premium on objective, transparent, accountable, equitable, and inclusive procedures. The entirely digital process is designed to minimize opportunities for manipulation or seat-selling practices.

Indonesia's Ombudsman has announced it is ready to fully monitor SPMB 2026 nationwide, including Bali, to ensure procedural compliance and to address any complaints from the public. Independent oversight adds a further layer of accountability to the process.

## How to Register at bali.spmb.id

Here is a step-by-step guide to completing your SPMB registration:

1. **Visit** bali.spmb.id from a browser on any device
2. **Create an account** using your National Student ID Number (NISN) and personal data matching your graduation certificate
3. **Upload required documents**: graduation certificate or letter of graduation (SKL), Family Card (KK), and recent passport-size photo
4. **Select your pathway** and choose your desired school
5. **Print** the registration confirmation as proof of enrollment

Parents and students are advised to prepare all documents well in advance and to verify that personal data in the National Education Data System (Dapodik) matches their civil registration records. Discrepancies can cause registration attempts to fail during verification.

Announcement of selection results will be released in stages according to each pathway's timeline. Students can monitor available school quotas, selection status, and final announcements directly through the bali.spmb.id portal.
