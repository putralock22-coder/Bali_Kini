Bali's **Pekan Olahraga Pelajar (PORJAR)** — the Student Sports Week — officially begins on **June 5–10, 2026** across Denpasar and multiple venues throughout the Island of the Gods. This prestigious biennial multi-sport event once again serves as a grand stage for thousands of Bali's finest young athletes to compete for glory at the provincial level.

---

## What Is PORJAR Bali?

PORJAR is a provincial-level multi-sport competition exclusively for elementary, middle, and high school students across Bali. The event aims to identify promising young athletic talent while fostering sportsmanship and inter-regional unity.

PORJAR Bali 2026 features **39 official sports disciplines** plus **3 exhibition sports**, making it one of the largest student sports events in Eastern Indonesia.

---

## Contingents from Across Bali Field Thousands of Athletes

Every regency and city in Bali has dispatched its best contingent. Here is a breakdown of the competing delegations:

### Badung Regency — Gunning for the Overall Title
Badung fields the largest contingent with a total of **1,707 people**, comprising **1,441 athletes** and **266 coaches and officials**. The Regent of Badung officially sent off the contingent with a clear target: clinching the **overall champion title at PORJAR Bali 2026** — an ambition backed by years of sustained athlete development investment.

### Tabanan Regency — 801 Warriors
Tabanan sent **801 people** including **648 athletes**, **125 coaches and officials**, and **28 medical and support staff**. The inclusion of a full medical team reflects Tabanan's commitment to keeping their athletes in peak condition throughout the competition.

### Klungkung Regency — 653 Athletes in 31 Sports
Klungkung dispatched **653 athletes** competing across **31 of the 39 sports** on offer. Regent I Made Satria personally saw off the contingent, expressing confidence in achieving better results than previous editions.

### Gianyar Regency — 590 Athletes Ready to Compete
Gianyar demonstrated its strength as a student sports powerhouse by sending **590 selected athletes** who went through rigorous district- and regency-level selection.

### Jembrana Regency — Targeting the Top Five
Jembrana sent a delegation of **414 people** consisting of **352 athletes** and **62 coaches**. Regent I Made Kembang set an ambitious but realistic target: finishing in the **top five** in the overall standings — a goal backed by months of intensive preparation.

### Bangli Regency — Optimistic About Improvement
Bangli dispatched **290 combined personnel** including athletes, coaches, and officials. The Deputy Regent of Bangli expressed confidence that the regency's athletes would deliver significantly improved results compared to the previous PORJAR.

### Buleleng Regency — Taekwondo Specialists
From Buleleng, **22 top taekwondo athletes** are poised to compete for medals, making them among the frontrunners in the martial arts discipline.

## Sports on the Programme

PORJAR Bali 2026 features **39 official competitive sports** spanning a wide range of disciplines, including:

- **Team sports**: football, volleyball, basketball, doubles badminton
- **Individual sports**: athletics, swimming, taekwondo, pencak silat, karate
- **Traditional sports** reflecting Bali's rich local heritage
- **3 exhibition disciplines** showcasing emerging sports gaining traction among young Balinese

---

## More Than Just Competition

PORJAR is far more than a medal hunt. It functions as a **sustained athlete development ecosystem**. Young athletes who excel at PORJAR become candidates for sports scholarships, gain access to priority school enrollment pathways, and may represent Bali at the National Sports Week (PON).

From a tourism and local economy standpoint, PORJAR also delivers a positive multiplier effect — thousands of athletes, coaches, families, and supporters converging on Denpasar drive demand for accommodation, food, and local transportation.

---

## Schedule and Venues

PORJAR Bali 2026 runs from **Thursday, June 5** through **Tuesday, June 10, 2026**, with competitions spread across various venues in and around Denpasar. Detailed schedules for each sport can be obtained through the education and sports offices of each respective regency.
