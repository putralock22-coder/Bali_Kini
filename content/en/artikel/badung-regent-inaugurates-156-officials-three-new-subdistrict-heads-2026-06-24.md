## Badung Regency Refreshes Its Leadership Structure

Badung Regent I Wayan Adi Arnawa officially inaugurated **156 officials** within the Badung Regency Government (Pemkab Badung), including filling **three new subdistrict head (camat)** positions, in a ceremony held at the Kertha Gosana Room of the Puspem Badung complex on Wednesday (24/6/2026).

This inauguration represents one of the largest organizational refreshes conducted by the Badung administration in the first half of 2026 — part of an effort to strengthen bureaucratic performance and ensure the right personnel are placed in positions matching their competencies and regional needs.

## Three New Subdistrict Heads

### Kuta Utara Subdistrict Head
The Kuta Utara subdistrict head position is now held by **I Nyoman Tirtayasa**, previously serving as Head of the Regional Tax UPTD for Kuta Utara at the Badung Regional Revenue Agency (Bapenda). Kuta Utara encompasses strategic tourism areas including Kerobokan, Canggu, and Tibubeneng — some of Badung's fastest-growing tourism zones in recent years.

### Kuta Subdistrict Head
The Kuta subdistrict head post was handed to **I Made Agus Suantara**, who previously served as the Deputy Head (Sekcam) of Kuta. Kuta Subdistrict is the heart of Bali's tourism industry, encompassing Kuta, Legian, and Seminyak — areas with the highest density of tourist activity across the entire island.

### Abiansemal Subdistrict Head Position Remains Vacant

Despite 156 officials being sworn in, the **Abiansemal subdistrict head** position remains unfilled. This gap is of particular note given that Abiansemal has significant potential for rural tourism development within Badung Regency.

## Regent's Message: Act Fast, Stay Field-Oriented

In his address, Regent Adi Arnawa delivered firm direction to all newly sworn-in officials to act quickly and maintain a field-oriented service approach.

"We are moving fast to place our best talents to strengthen our bureaucratic institutions going forward," stated Regent Arnawa. He also emphasized the importance of new officials immediately familiarizing themselves with ground-level conditions in their respective work areas — particularly regarding tourism, investment, and public services.

## Context: Badung, Bali's Highest Revenue Regency

Badung Regency is recognized as Bali's highest-earning regency in terms of Regional Own-Source Revenue (PAD), largely driven by tourism across Kuta, Seminyak, Canggu, and Nusa Dua. Given this strategic position, leadership at the subdistrict level is critically important for maintaining a conducive, orderly, and sustainable tourism ecosystem.

Filling the camat positions in Kuta and Kuta Utara — the two subdistricts with the highest tourism activity — was a priority given the growing complexity of challenges in these areas, from traffic management and street vendor regulation to oversight of the constantly expanding accommodation and hospitality sector.

This mass inauguration is expected to serve as a catalyst for accelerating development and improving public service quality across Badung Regency, in line with its vision of becoming a regency with a professional bureaucracy and world-class tourism industry.
