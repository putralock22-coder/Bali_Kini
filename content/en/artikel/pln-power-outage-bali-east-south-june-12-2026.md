PT PLN (Persero) Unit Induk Distribusi (UID) Bali has scheduled electrical network maintenance across several areas of Bali on Friday, June 12, 2026. The maintenance work will temporarily interrupt electricity supply in certain areas, particularly those under the service jurisdiction of PLN UP3 East Bali and PLN UP3 South Bali.

## Affected Areas

According to PLN UID Bali, the two main service units affected are:

- **PLN UP3 East Bali** — covering the Klungkung Regency, Gianyar Regency, and surrounding areas
- **PLN UP3 South Bali** — covering South Denpasar, Kuta, Legian, and other key tourism zones

Residents in these regions should be prepared for temporary disruptions to electricity supply during the hours when maintenance activities are being carried out.

## Purpose of the Maintenance

PLN stated that the maintenance is being conducted to improve the reliability of the power distribution network and maintain the quality of electricity supply to all customers across Bali. This is part of PLN's routine infrastructure upkeep program, carried out at regular intervals throughout the year.

### Long-Term Benefits

Regular network maintenance is intended to:

1. Prevent more severe, unscheduled breakdowns
2. Ensure stable voltage and consistent power quality
3. Extend the operational lifespan of distribution equipment
4. Minimize the risk of unexpected power failures

## Advice for Residents and Businesses

PLN urges customers in the affected areas to adjust activities requiring electricity during the maintenance window. Key recommendations include:

- Save important work on electronic devices before the scheduled outage
- Prepare backup lighting sources such as flashlights or candles
- Businesses with critical power needs should consider using generators or uninterruptible power supply (UPS) units

It should be noted that the maintenance schedule is subject to change at any time based on technical conditions on the ground. For the latest information and specific schedules by village or neighborhood, residents are advised to monitor PLN UID Bali's official channels.

## How to Contact PLN

For further information or to report outages outside the scheduled window, customers can reach PLN through:

- **Phone: 123** (24-hour customer service)
- **PLN Mobile App**
- **Instagram:** @plndistribusibali
- **Instagram (East Bali unit):** @plnbalitimur

Routine network maintenance of this kind reflects PLN's ongoing commitment to ensuring reliable electricity across Bali, including in tourist-heavy zones that have exceptionally high year-round power demands.
