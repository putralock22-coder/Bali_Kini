Bali has made history on the global tourism stage. For the **first time ever**, the Island of the Gods has been crowned the **world's best travel destination** by the *TripAdvisor Travelers' Choice Awards: Best of the Best Destinations 2026* — surpassing a lineup of iconic cities that have long dominated the list, including London, Dubai, Paris, and Rome.

This prestigious recognition is based on the analysis of **millions of traveler reviews and ratings** collected worldwide between **October 1, 2024 and September 30, 2025**, covering hotel stays, restaurant dining, and activities listed on the TripAdvisor platform.

## Defeating the World's Toughest Competition

In TripAdvisor's *Best of the Best Destinations* 2026 ranking, Bali tops the **global top 10** list as follows:

1. **Bali, Indonesia** 🥇
2. Mauritius
3. Maldives
4. St. Lucia
5. Galle, Sri Lanka
6. Hue, Vietnam
7. Napa, United States
8. Positano, Italy
9. Watamu, Kenya
10. Antigua

Bali successfully dethroned **London**, the previous title holder, while also outranking Dubai, Hanoi, Paris, and Rome — destinations that have dominated global tourism rankings for years.

## More Than Just Beaches

TripAdvisor describes Bali as *"a living postcard admired for its stunning lush landscapes and white sand beaches."* But this recognition goes far beyond natural beauty. Bali was evaluated comprehensively — its cultural depth and spirituality, culinary diversity, accommodation quality, and the warmth of its people all played key roles.

Beyond claiming the No. 1 world destination title, Bali also earned:
- **World's Best Honeymoon Destination** (No. 1 globally)
- **World's Best Solo Travel Destination** (No. 9 globally)

## Official Reactions: Pride and Responsibility

**Indonesia's Minister of Tourism, Widiyanti Putri Wardhana**, expressed deep appreciation for the trust and positive evaluations from travelers worldwide. "This recognition proves that Bali continues to be a global tourism magnet. International feedback like this is invaluable in shaping the direction of Bali's future tourism development," she stated.

**Bali Governor Wayan Koster** attributed this achievement to Bali's authentic strengths, linking the success to Bali's **culture, traditions, arts, and natural environment**. For Koster, the award also aligns with the Bali government's ongoing push for quality tourism transformation — not merely chasing visitor numbers, but attracting travelers who truly appreciate what Bali has to offer.

## A Historic Achievement Built on Authentic Reviews

For years, Bali has consistently ranked among TripAdvisor's top 10 global destinations, but the top spot always remained out of reach. Now, after building a solid reputation from millions of authentic reviews by global travelers, Bali has finally written a new chapter in its history.

TripAdvisor's methodology does not use editorial selections or surveys — it is based purely on the **quality and quantity of genuine reviews** from travelers who have actually visited and experienced each destination. This makes the award one of the most credible and representative recognitions in the global tourism industry.

## A Momentum for Sustainable Tourism

For Bali, this title arrives at a pivotal time. The Bali Provincial Government is actively promoting the concept of *quality tourism* — prioritizing the quality of visitor experiences, environmental sustainability, and respect for Balinese adat (customary) traditions.

With its new status as TripAdvisor's No. 1 destination in the world, Bali hopes to attract more quality-conscious travelers — those who come not just for beaches and photos, but who are genuinely ready to appreciate, protect, and positively contribute to the preservation of the island's authentic soul.
