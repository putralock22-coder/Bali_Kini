## Bali Opens SMA/SMK School Admissions Phase II

The Bali Provincial Education, Youth, and Sports Office (Disdikpora) officially opened the **Student Admission System (SPMB) for SMA/SMK Phase II** starting Monday, **June 30, 2026**, with registration running through **July 2, 2026**. This second phase opens a second opportunity for prospective students who were not admitted in Phase I to register through the domicile (residency-based) pathway.

This phase provides students who didn't make it through Phase I selection with another chance to compete for a place at their preferred school based on their residential location.

## A Unique Innovation: Krama Desa Adat Pathway

One of the most distinctive features of Bali's 2026 SPMB — setting it apart from school admission systems in other Indonesian regions — is the inclusion of a **Special Krama Desa Adat Pathway**, which receives a dedicated **5 percent quota** from each school's total enrollment capacity.

This pathway is specifically designed to strengthen the role and presence of traditional villages (*desa adat*) in Bali's social and educational life. Prospective students choosing this pathway must demonstrate that they are active members of the local *krama desa adat* (customary village community).

### Requirements for the Krama Desa Adat Pathway

Students applying through this pathway must submit:

1. **A letter from the Bendesa Adat** (customary village head) — using the official letterhead format, signed by the Bendesa Adat on official stamp paper
2. **Family Card (Kartu Keluarga)** issued according to registration timing requirements
3. **Academic Ability Test Certificate (SHTKA)** as proof of academic competency
4. **Accumulated report card grades** from semesters 1 through 5

The quota allocated to each school varies — it is adjusted proportionally to the number of learning groups (rombel) and the capacity of each educational institution.

Prospective students and parents are advised to carefully note the schedule and ensure all required documents are prepared well before the registration deadline.

## Background: Bali's 2026 SPMB System

SPMB 2026 replaces the previously used PPDB (New Student Enrollment) system, introducing several refinements in the selection mechanism. One of the most significant changes is the use of the **Academic Ability Test (TKA)** as the primary selection criterion, replacing the national exam scores that were previously used.

The system is designed to promote equitable access to quality education while honoring the diverse admission pathways that reflect Bali's unique social and cultural context. The krama desa adat pathway is a concrete recognition of the vital role that traditional institutions play in Balinese community life.

For prospective students who have not yet registered or were unsuccessful in Phase I, SPMB Phase II represents the last opportunity to secure a place in their preferred SMA/SMK before the 2026/2027 academic year begins.

Registration information is available through the official Bali Province SPMB portal at **spmb.baliprov.go.id**.
