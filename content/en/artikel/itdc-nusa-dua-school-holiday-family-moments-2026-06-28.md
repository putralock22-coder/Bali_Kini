Bali's premier managed beach enclave, **The Nusa Dua**, operated by PT InJourney Tourism Development Corporation (ITDC), is rolling out an extensive programme to welcome families during the 2026 school holiday season. Under the theme *"Comfortable Holiday Full of Meaning,"* the destination is offering a rich blend of family activities, cultural experiences, and exclusive hotel promotions.

## The Star Attraction: Family Moments in Paradise

At the heart of this year's school holiday programme is **Family Moments in Paradise** — a curated series of themed activities designed to engage every member of the family, from young children to grandparents.

### Memory Lane Playground at Peninsula Island

The centrepiece of the programme is the **Memory Lane Playground**, set on the exclusive Peninsula Island within The Nusa Dua's pristine grounds. This immersive play space invites families to:

- **Art classes** – traditional Balinese painting, batik dyeing, and nature-based crafts
- **Interactive games** – themed play zones tailored for different age groups
- **Cultural activities** – hands-on introduction to Balinese traditions, including canang (floral offering) making
- **Wildlife exhibit** – an educational showcase of Indonesia's native fauna

The playground goes beyond entertainment, embedding Balinese culture and environmental awareness into every activity.

### Picnic with Parents and Family Time Capsule

Additional highlights include a **Picnic with Parents** event on Nusa Dua's tranquil beachfront, complete with local Balinese cuisine and interactive family games. The **Family Time Capsule** activity invites families to document and preserve their holiday memories in a creative keepsake, encouraging reflection on what it means to travel together with purpose.

A dedicated programme for **National Children's Day** rounds out the calendar, making The Nusa Dua a meaningful venue for Indonesia's annual celebration of youth.

## Participating Hotels and Exclusive Deals

Several of The Nusa Dua's iconic properties are offering bespoke school holiday promotions, giving families across the budget spectrum a way to experience this world-class destination:

- **The Westin Resort Nusa Dua** – family staycation packages with pool access and wellness programmes
- **The Laguna, A Luxury Collection Resort & Spa** – private lagoon snorkelling and resort-exclusive activities
- **Kayumanis Nusa Dua** – intimate villa stays with a Balinese cultural retreat experience
- **Courtyard by Marriott Bali Nusa Dua Resort** – family-friendly packages with a dedicated day camp for children
- **Renaissance Bali Nusa Dua Resort** – all-inclusive options with water sports facilities
- **Grand Whiz Hotel Nusa Dua** – accessible pricing within a premium-managed zone
- **Amarterra Villas Bali Nusa Dua** – private villas steeped in authentic Balinese aesthetics

All hotel promotions are valid throughout the semester school break 2026.

## Why The Nusa Dua Stands Apart

As an exclusively managed tourism estate, The Nusa Dua offers families something increasingly rare: a controlled, clean, and beautifully maintained environment where safety and comfort are guaranteed. The combination of pristine white-sand beaches, crystal-clear water, internationally certified hospitality, and now a packed activity calendar makes it one of the most complete family destinations in Southeast Asia.

ITDC is running similar programmes at **The Mandalika** in West Nusa Tenggara and **The Golo Mori** in East Nusa Tenggara, reflecting the corporation's broader strategy of elevating managed tourism zones across Indonesia's outer islands.

The 2026 school holidays mark a strong opportunity for families to experience the very best of Bali — enriched by cultural discovery, luxury at various price points, and memories that will last far beyond the summer break.
