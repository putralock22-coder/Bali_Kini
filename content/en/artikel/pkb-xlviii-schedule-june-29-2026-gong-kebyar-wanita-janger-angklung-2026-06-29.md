The 48th Bali Arts Festival (Pesta Kesenian Bali — PKB XLVIII) 2026 brings another full day of captivating performances on Monday, June 29, 2026. Tonight is particularly anticipated by traditional music lovers: a much-anticipated Gong Kebyar Wanita showdown pitting the delegations of Klungkung Regency against Tabanan Regency at the iconic open-air Ardha Candra Stage inside the Taman Budaya (Art Center) complex in Denpasar.

## Full Schedule: PKB June 29, 2026

Here is the complete program for today:

### 3:00 PM WITA — Angklung Kebyar: Buleleng vs Tabanan
**Kalangan Ratna Kanda**

The afternoon kicks off with the Utsawa (Parade) Angklung Kebyar contest, bringing together Sekaa Angklung Kebyar Shanti Ulangun from Buleleng Regency and Sekaa Angklung Merta Sari from Tabanan Regency. Angklung is a distinctive Balinese gamelan ensemble tuned to the selendro scale, producing resonant, spiritually charged tones. Inter-regency competitions like this one draw devoted audiences who appreciate the subtle differences in style and expression each ensemble brings to the same repertoire.

### 5:00 PM WITA — Lenggak Lenggok Betawi (Jakarta)
**Kalangan Angsoka**

The afternoon performance slot at Kalangan Angsoka welcomes a multicultural guest: Sanggar Seni Betawi Ratna Sari from East Jakarta, presenting "Lenggak Lenggok Betawi" — a showcase of traditional Betawi (Jakarta) dance and music. The inclusion of arts groups from outside Bali underlines PKB's identity as a national arts festival that celebrates Indonesia's full cultural diversity, not just Balinese traditions.

### 7:00 PM WITA — Janger Tradisi Remaja: Gianyar
**Kalangan Ayodya**

After dark, Kalangan Ayodya hosts the Utsawa Janger Tradisi by Sekaa Janger Galuci from Gianyar Regency. Janger is a beloved Balinese vocal art form in which two groups — young men (sekaa truna) and young women (sekaa truni) — perform alternating verses and songs accompanied by gamelan music. Tonight's performance doubles as a generational showcase, highlighting young Balinese artists carrying forward this cherished tradition.

### 7:30 PM WITA — Gong Kebyar Wanita: Klungkung vs Tabanan
**Panggung Terbuka Ardha Candra**

The night's main event is the Utsawa Gong Kebyar Wanita competition between two of Bali's most celebrated all-women gamelan ensembles:
- **Sekaa Gong Kebyar Wanita Komunitas Wakul Mas** — Klungkung Regency
- **Sekaa Gong Istri Kesari Mesari 108** — Tabanan Regency

Gong Kebyar Wanita is one of PKB's most prestigious categories, featuring entirely female-led gamelan ensembles who perform selected tabuh compositions and accompanying dances before a panel of judges and thousands of spectators at the Ardha Candra open-air stage.

## PKB 2026: Entering Its Fourth Week

PKB XLVIII 2026 has been running since June 13 and continues through July 11, 2026 under the theme **"Atma Kerthi: Jiwa Sidha Parisudha"** — a Balinese philosophical concept celebrating the purification of the soul and the essence of human existence.

This year's edition brings together more than 23,000 artists from across Bali and beyond. The Taman Budaya Art Center complex in Denpasar is not only a stage for artistic performances but also a thriving creative economy hub, with hundreds of UMKM (small business) stalls offering local food, crafts, and traditional products to tens of thousands of daily visitors.

### Visitor Tips for Tonight

Those planning to attend the Gong Kebyar Wanita performance at Ardha Candra are advised to arrive by 6:30 PM WITA to secure good seating. General admission to the Taman Budaya grounds is free. While parking is available nearby, using ride-hailing apps or public transport is strongly recommended given the large evening crowds.

Tonight's lineup promises an unforgettable night of authentic Balinese performing arts — a golden opportunity for both visitors and local residents to experience PKB at its finest.
