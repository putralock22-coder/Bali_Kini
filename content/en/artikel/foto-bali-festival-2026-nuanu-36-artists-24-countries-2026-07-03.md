**FOTO Bali Festival 2026** is approaching its final days. The international photography festival presented by **Nuanu Creative City** runs from **June 3 to July 12, 2026** — and for lovers of visual art, only a handful of days remain to experience it in person.

This second edition of the festival brings together **36 artists** from **24 countries**, selected from nearly **700 submissions** received from more than **80 countries** worldwide. The competitive selection process alone signals how powerfully Bali is establishing itself as a serious international stage for contemporary visual art.

## Theme: Afterimage — Photography as Memory

The festival's overarching theme is **"Afterimage"** — a concept that invites viewers to reflect on how an image continues to resonate in memory long after the captured moment has passed.

Curated by **Kurniadi Widodo** and **Putu Sridiniari**, the exhibited works explore photography across multiple dimensions: as **memory**, as **evidence**, as **trace**, as **archive**, and as **speculation about the future**. This unconventional curatorial approach elevates FOTO Bali Festival far beyond a standard photo exhibition — it is a layered, immersive conversation about the role of images in shaping how we perceive the world and ourselves.

## Eight Indonesian Artists Represent the Local Voice

Among the 36 selected artists, **eight are from Indonesia**, bringing a local perspective that enriches the cross-cultural dialogue unfolding at Nuanu. Their inclusion — particularly those with roots in Bali — affirms that the festival is not only a platform for international voices, but also a meaningful space that celebrates the growing recognition of Indonesian photography on the world stage.

Works from all participating artists are displayed across the various exhibition spaces of Nuanu Creative City, located in **Tabanan** regency. The venue's lush natural setting and organic architecture are deliberately woven into the overall artistic experience — Nuanu was designed as an ecosystem that fuses creativity, nature, and community.

## 40 Days of Public Programming

Over its 40-day run, FOTO Bali Festival 2026 goes well beyond static wall displays. An extensive program of public events has been open to all throughout the festival:

- **Artist talks and panel discussions** exploring the creative processes behind the works and the Afterimage concept
- **Photography workshops** catering to beginners and professionals alike
- **Film screenings** thematically linked to memory and visual imagery
- **Open-air projections** of artworks on Nuanu's grounds after dark

### Bali Consolidates Its Role as Southeast Asia's Photography Hub

The success of FOTO Bali Festival's second edition strengthens Bali's position as the leading photography hub in Southeast Asia. The festival aligns directly with Bali's provincial government vision of building **quality tourism** — not chasing visitor numbers, but attracting travelers with genuine depth of interest in art, culture, and authentic experience.

For those yet to visit, Nuanu Creative City is reachable in approximately 45 minutes from Kuta or Seminyak heading west toward Tabanan. There is still time until **July 12, 2026** to witness one of Southeast Asia's most significant photography festivals before it closes.
